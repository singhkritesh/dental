Payer: Cigna Dental
Plan Snapshot (sample reference):
- Preventive services: covered at 100%
- Basic services: covered at 70%
- Endodontic services, including root canal therapy (RCT): covered at 70% after deductible
- Major services: covered at 50%
- Orthodontics: not covered in this sample plan
- Annual maximum: $1,250
- Waiting periods: 6 months for basic, 12 months for major
- Prior authorization: required for selected oral surgery and high-cost prosthodontics
- Exclusions/limitations: cosmetic veneers not covered, implants limited by plan rider
