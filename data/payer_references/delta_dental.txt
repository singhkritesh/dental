Payer: Delta Dental
Plan Snapshot (sample reference):
- Preventive exams and cleanings: covered at 100%, no deductible
- Basic restorative services: covered at 80% after deductible
- Endodontic services, including root canal therapy (RCT): covered at 80% after deductible
- Major restorative services (crowns, bridges): covered at 50% after deductible
- Annual maximum: $1,500
- Waiting period for major services: 12 months for new members
- Prior authorization: recommended for major services over $750
- Common exclusions: cosmetic-only procedures, lost appliance replacement more than once per 5 years
